In your life, you must have come across or, are presently in a situation which demands too much of you. Too much cognitive bandwidth. Too much pre-requisite knowledge. Too much practical exposure. 

You might feel like it's the end of the world as you spend every hour sipping a cocktail of uncertainty and imposter syndrome, making you feel like they will *find out* just any moment now that you're *not enough*.

Take a step back and try to remember. When you feel like that, what are you fearing? Not knowing enough or, not **being enough**?

Try to remember and answer. If it's the first one, then, I'm going to tell you something that won't change your life but how it happens to you. 

Just one thing. Even when your mind is racing and you don't know what to say or think, say just one thing. 

*yet.*

You don't know enough about ML *yet*. You haven't found a 0-day in Chrome *yet*. You haven't built a successful startup, *yet*.

And yet, you will. 

However, if your answer was the second one, that's a thought for another day.  
For now remember,

>All is built on sand but we must build as if the sand were stone. And even if it’s not your ideal life, you can always choose it.
